About us

Our vision

Our school's vision is to create a community of learners who are passionate about their education, their personal growth, and their role in making the world a better place. We strive to be a leader in innovative teaching and learning practices that challenge and engage our students, inspiring them to become lifelong learners who are creative, critical thinkers and problem-solvers. Our vision is to create an environment that encourages curiosity, exploration, and risk-taking, where students are empowered to take ownership of their learning and are supported in their pursuit of their passions and interests. We envision our students as leaders who are grounded in their values and committed to using their knowledge and skills to make a positive impact on the world around them. We are dedicated to creating a culture of excellence that celebrates diversity, equity, and inclusion, and prepares our students to be compassionate, responsible, and global citizens.

Our mission

At our school, our mission is to provide a nurturing and challenging learning environment that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential. We believe in fostering a love of learning that extends beyond the classroom and instilling in our students a sense of responsibility, empathy, and respect for themselves, others, and the world around them. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to creating a safe and inclusive community that values diversity, equity, and inclusion, and encourages critical thinking, creativity, and innovation. We are dedicated to preparing our students to be global citizens who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in a rapidly changing world and to make a positive impact on society.

About SCHOLAR ROSEMARY SCHOOL

Starting its voyage from 20 April 1994, Scholar Rosemary, New Delhi, has emerged as a pioneering institute on the horizon of Edu-dom to develop individuals with razor sharp abilities with the constant effort of the students ,timely guidance from Rosemary International, cooperation from parents and community, unflinching sincerity and unflagging devotion of its dedicated staff. The School is Affiliated to the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education), Delhi up to class XII wide.
